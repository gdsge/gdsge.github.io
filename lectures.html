An Introduction to GDSGE, 7/7/2020 at PHBS

This lecture introduces the concept of global solutions, the GDSGE framework, and the basic usage of the toolbox through three examples: A Simple RBC Model, Bianchi (2011), and Kiyotaki and Moore (1997) with Risk-averse Agents.
